About Roomly

Our mission is to simplify the rental process for both landlords and renters. By eliminating the need for hefty deposits, we make finding a new home more affordable for tenants while ensuring landlords have a hassle-free experience. With a straightforward subscription fee of just £10, we bring transparency and convenience to the rental market.

Features of Roomly

Explore the unique features that make Roomly the best choice for landlords and renters alike.

  • No Deposits: Say goodbye to large upfront costs. Renters can secure their next home with a simple subscription fee.
  • Easy Listings: Landlords can quickly list properties and reach a wide audience of potential renters.

  • 24/7 Support: Our dedicated support team is available around the clock to assist with any queries or issues.
