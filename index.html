Easy To Setup and Use

How Roomly Works?

Roomly connects landlords and renters in an efficient, seamless, and secure way. Our platform leverages advanced technology to make property rental simple and transparent for everyone.

List or Browse

Landlords list their properties with detailed descriptions and photos. Renters browse through available listings to find their perfect match.

Connect

Renters express interest in properties, and landlords review applications.

Contract & Subscription

Renters sign a contract with the landlord and pay a small monthly subscription fee of £9.99 in addition to the agreed-upon rent. Payments are securely made online.

Move In

Once everything is finalized, renters can move into their new home without any deposit.
